Changes save to flash. The radar refreshes the moment you tap Save.

◎Radarcenter, range, units▸

Decimal degrees, comma-separated. This is the center of the radar and the source for auto-timezone.

Range accepts a unit (mi, km, nm; default mi) and snaps to the nearest preset: 2, 3, 6, 8, 10, 20, or 30 mi.

☼Display & screensbrightness, timeouts, clock▸

Auto timezone resolves the local zone from your radar lat/lon over Wi‑Fi. Turn the clock-settings knob on the device to override manually.

✈Route APIsairline & route lookup▸

Enabled providers run in order — FlightAware → AirLabs → FR24 — and the first hit per callsign wins. Paste multiple keys comma-separated (key1, key2, key3); when one hits its monthly cap the next is used before moving to the next provider. Leave a field blank to keep the saved value. Caps reset on the 1st once NTP time syncs.
